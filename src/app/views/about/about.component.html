What is Pixi?

Pixi is social network oriented to multimedia consumption submitted by the user and general discussion. Pixi was created by Italo Russo. On Pixi, "Personas" are the first-class citizens. A Persona can post, vote, comment and create channels.

User accounts can have one or multiple Personas. Channels are managed by their respective Persona (mods). They establish rules and are in their power to ban any Persona who doesn't comply with them.

A Mod Persona on a channel can ban another Persona and its user account, so no other Persona from that account can post, but the Mod Persona cannot see the owner of the Persona or its other Personas. This ensures strict anonymity with everyone, including staff members.

Pixi is built around Freedom of Speech. Our utmost important value is to protect your voice. We try our best to ensure your ideas will be shown and heard.

However, to ensure the wellbeing of the whole community and harmony between channels and users, we shall enforce a short but strict list of rules, known as the Global Rules.

Global Rules
  1. NSFW content MUST always be marked as such.
  2. NSFW channels (Porn, morbid curiosity, triggering stuff) MUST always be marked as such.
  3. No child pornography, or any other banned content according to the U.S. Law for that matter.
  4. Content MUST always be relevant to the topic of the channel.
  5. Ban avoidance *will* result in a permanent ban of any account.
  6. Staff impersonation is strictly forbidden.
  7. We encourage our users to deliver good/high quality content. This is quite subjective, therefore we do not enforce it. Just something to be aware of.

Global Rules apply to all channels. Violation of some of these rules may result in a permanent ban for the user or Persona, and/or content deletion.

Some Technical Policies

Pixi allows for images and videos uploads with certain limitations. Images are restricted to PNG, JPG, BMP and GIF of up to 10 megabytes. Videos on the other hand are restricted to WEBM and MP4 of up to also 10 megabytes but 4 minutes time limit, too. A post is limited to 10 images or 5 videos. A post can consist of images and videos, you must have image a video is equivalent to 2 images. This means a post can have 2 videos and 5 images, or 3 videos and 4 images, and so on.

Channels can be set up so only a specific type of content is allowed. For example, a channel can disable audio on its videos. A video uploaded to this kind of channel will be stripped off of its audio completely.
